# Game Title 

**Short Description:**  
This is an isometric 2.5D exploration and adventure game where you control a character moving through layered terrain, gathering resources, and interacting with various elements in a lively isometric world.

##  World

In this crimson-hued realm of ruin and redemption, you stand as the last scion of a forgotten royal bloodline. The Empire—once a beacon of power—now teeters on the edge of oblivion, besieged by twisted creatures bearing glowing relics of an ancient, evil god. Across sprawling isometric battlefields, you will discover and master the mysterious segis and arches, unlocking new strengths and long-dormant sorceries. From sun-scorched deserts to crumbling fortress-cities, a world stained in shades of red invites you to defy the cult that seeks to resurrect the shattered deity, even as you grapple with your own destiny. Will you safeguard the Empire’s future or dare to break the cycle of chaos forever? The choice—and the fate of this dying land—lies in your hands.

## **Overview**
**Genre:** Isometric Action RPG  
**Platforms:** PC (Windows/Mac), potentially next-gen consoles  
**Engine:** Unreal Engine / Unity (TBD)  
**Art Style:** Dark fantasy with a reddish hue

**High Concept**  
Crimson Echoes is an isometric action RPG where you harness the power of “segis” and “arches” to fight monstrosities born from a shattered god’s essence. Play as the last heir of an ancient royal bloodline, exploring cursed lands to save or reshape a crumbling Empire.

---

## **Story & Setting**
1. **Ancient Evil:** A forbidden god once roamed these lands, created by the “Followers of Primordial Physics.” Though defeated, its fragments (segis) still corrupt the realm.  
2. **Royal Legacy:** You, the player, inherit a lost lineage tied to this sealed evil—uniquely resistant to the god’s corruption.  
3. **Crimson Lands:** The world is steeped in ominous red tones, reflecting the unending threat of the shattered deity’s influence.  
4. **Conflict of Ideals:** Tensions rise between rebuilding the Empire and stopping the cult’s plan to resurrect the ancient god.

---

## **Key Features**
- **Isometric Exploration:** Classic top-down perspective with in-depth environmental storytelling.  
- **Artifact-Based Progression:** Collect and master segis to enhance physical/magical abilities; arches awaken deeper powers.  
- **Action-Oriented Combat:** Swift melee, ranged attacks, and arcane abilities in real-time battles against mutated creatures and cultists.  
- **Narrative Depth:** Engage in story-rich quests that challenge moral choices—save the Empire or dismantle it for a new dawn.  
- **Dynamic Allies & Factions:** Encounter wanderers, former Imperial knights, and merchants; forge alliances or face betrayals.

---

## **Gameplay Mechanics**
- **Controls & Movement:** Fluid WASD or point-and-click style with dodge and block options.  
- **Combat System:** Fast-paced, combining melee combos, ranged tactics, and spells from segis or arch-derived powers.  
- **Skill Progression:** Multiple trees (Physical Mastery, Arcane Path, Royal Bloodline) unlocked through experience and artifact trials.  
- **Trials & Puzzles:** Overcome spirits guarding arches in challenge rooms, testing combat prowess and wit.  
- **Settlement / Fort Management (Optional):** Restore captured castles, recruit allies, and bolster the Empire’s defenses.

---

## **Characters & Factions**
- **Player Character (The Heir):** Royal lineage with a unique resistance to segis corruption.  
- **Father (Retired Warrior):** Mentor figure, teaching you to harness segis safely.  
- **Allies:** Wanderers, disillusioned Empire officials, and mercenaries drawn to your cause.  
- **Cult of Primordial Physics:** Villainous sect seeking to unite segis fragments to resurrect the forbidden god.  
- **Spirits (Arch Guardians):** Elemental or conceptual entities testing your worthiness to wield their gifts.

---

## **World & Level Design**
- **Regions:** Border settlements, ruined fortresses, and cursed landscapes saturated in reds and dark hues.  
- **Isometric Layering:** Ramps, stairs, crumbling bridges—environmental variety to enhance verticality.  
- **Exploration Focus:** Hidden segis shards, lore documents, and puzzle-driven progression.

---

## **Progression & Rewards**
1. **Core Loop:** Explore → Defeat monsters → Collect segis / artifacts → Upgrade skills → Face bosses → Advance story.  
2. **Settlement Upgrades (Optional):** Liberate forts to gain new allies, resources, and quest lines.  
3. **Character Growth:** XP-based level system plus specialized skill trees. Arches unlock rare “ultimate” abilities.

---

- **Character Design:**

## Features

- **Isometric World:**  
  Experience a rich environment composed of multiple terrain layers, allowing you to move not only horizontally and vertically, but also adjust your character’s position across layered blocks.

- **Dynamic Character Controls:**  
  Move freely using the WASD keys, jump up layers with the Spacebar, and descend layers with the Left Shift key. This layered movement adds depth and complexity to the standard 2D navigation.

- **Animated Tiles and Characters:**  
  Enjoy smooth animations for certain blocks (like waterfalls or special tiles) and for your character. The world feels more alive as animated sprites cycle through frames, adding visual interest.

- **Resource Gathering and Crafting (Planned):**  
  Collect resources from the environment and craft them into tools, weapons, and other helpful items. Future updates aim to introduce a deeper crafting system and expanded inventory management.

- **Enemies and Combat (Planned):**  
  Defend yourself against enemies inhabiting various layers of the map. Engage in combat with basic weapons to protect your resources and explore safely.

## Controls

- **Move:** W, A, S, D  
- **Jump Up a Layer:** Spacebar  
- **Move Down a Layer:** Left Shift  
- **Quit Game:** Close the window or press the appropriate quit shortcut (e.g., Alt+F4 on Windows)

## Getting Started

1. **Install Dependencies:**  
   Ensure `pygame` is installed. You can install it via:  
   ```bash
   pip install pygame
   ```

2. **Run the Game:**  
   From the `Game/` directory, run:  
   ```bash
   python main.py
   ```

3. **Explore and Interact:**  
   Move your character around, explore the map, and experiment with changing layers.

## File Structure

- **main.py**: The entry point of the game.  
- **config.py**: Contains game-wide configuration constants.  
- **modules/**: Contains modularized code for camera, world, player, and other systems.  
- **assets/**: Holds images, maps, and sound files used by the game.  
- **data/**: Contains game data such as items, recipes, and enemy definitions.  
- **logs/**: Stores game logs.
